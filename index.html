About Our Factory

Our wheat mill factory has been producing high-quality wheat products for over 50 years. We focus on providing the best grains for baking, cooking, and other culinary uses. Our state-of-the-art machinery ensures clean and nutritious wheat flour for all purposes.

Our Milling Process

We utilize advanced milling techniques to preserve the quality of the grain. The wheat undergoes several cleaning and grinding processes to remove impurities, ensuring that the final product is pure and healthy.
